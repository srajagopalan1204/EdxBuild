Welcome to the Scott Electric Training Site

This site hosts interactive training modules that support Scott Electric’s standard operating procedures (SOPs) for Electrical Distribution and specialty divisions such as Palco Power Systems.

Use these modules to practice key processes in a safe environment, build confidence with our ERP tools, and reduce surprises when working in the live system.

About Scott Electric

Scott Electric is a leading independent wholesale electrical distributor, serving electrical, mechanical, and general contractors, industrial customers, government agencies, and retail partners. The company provides a broad range of electrical products, lighting, tools, and related services to support both day-to-day operations and long-term projects.

The transformation of our information systems at Scott Electric has been made possible by the dedicated efforts of the ERP implementation team, a group of volunteer Champions, and the stewardship of our Executive Sponsor. The ongoing support and encouragement of the Management Team has been — and continues to be — a key source of inspiration for this work, driving continuous growth and improvement in how we serve our customers.

About this Training Site

The modules on this site use real-world examples to walk you through standard operating procedures. Most modules are interactive SOP players that mirror the screens and steps in our ERP system or related tools.

Practice these processes in the training environment to:

  • Become comfortable with key screens, fields, and decision points.
  • Reduce errors before working on live orders, inventory, or customers.
  • Build confidence when you are new to the company, the role, or the system.

The training environment is the best place to experiment, ask questions, and learn from mistakes — before they impact real customer or business situations.
